Dice Game

Dice game. Requires 2 players.

Instructions: Each player needs 100 points to win. The game starts with player 1. Player will click on "Roll Dice" to get a number between 1-6. The player then can choose to roll the dice again or click "Hold". Rolling the dice again will give another number between 1-6 (this adds to the previous number) and clicking on Hold will save the current points and then the next player will roll the dice.

If the player rolls the dice and gets a 1, the unsaved points will be lost and the turn will shift to the next player.
